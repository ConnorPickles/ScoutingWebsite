Welcome to my website! This website will give you an overview of what Venturers is all about. For those who do not know, Venturers is a section of scouting; it is the next level up from scouts. Venturers are all high school aged youth. These youth run much of their own programming, which is much different from any level of scouting below Venturers. Youth involved are expected to take some initiative,
and contribute to the group. This does mean that a Venture company does whatever the youth want, as long as it doesn’t break any laws!
There are advisors present that help the youth run their program and provide opportunities, and to keep youth in check. To learn more, navigate your way to another page of this website!
